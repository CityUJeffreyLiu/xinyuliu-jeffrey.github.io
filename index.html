About Me

Hello! My name is Xinyu Liu, 刘新宇 (in Chinese). I was born in Daqing, a small but beautiful city in Heilongjiang, China, in 1997. I am currently in the research group of Prof. YUAN Yixuan in the Department of Electrical Engineering of the City University of Hong Kong as a Ph.D. student. I received my M.Sc. degree with the Department of Control Science and Engineering, Harbin Institute of Technology and B.Sc. Degree from Honors School, Harbin Institute of Technology, supervised by Prof. Xiaoguang Di. I also had a wonderful internship in Deepwise AI Lab , supervised by Fandong Zhang and Prof. Yizhou Yu.

Research Interests

My research interests mainly include Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition, Medical Image Processing. I focus on object detection and segmentation tasks.
