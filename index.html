About Me

Hello! My name is Xinyu Liu, 刘新宇 (in Chinese). I was born in Daqing, a small but beautiful city in Heilongjiang, China, in 1997. I started my university life at the age of 17 in Harbin Institute of Technology. I am currently a M.Sc. student with the Department of Control Science and Engineering, Harbin Institute of Technology. I am supervised by Prof. Xiaoguang Di. I also had a wonderful internship in Deepwise AI Lab and supervised by Fandong Zhang and Prof. Yizhou Yu. Prior to joining Prof. Di's group to pursue my M.Sc. Degree, I recieved my B.Sc. Degree from Honors School of HIT in 2018.
